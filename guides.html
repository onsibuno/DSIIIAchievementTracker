Where to find Yhorm the Giant ?

Yhorm the Giant is found in the Profaned Capital, his room is the deepest in the capital

Where to find Aldrich, Devourer of Gods ?

Aldrich, Devourer of Gods is found in anor londo, in the last room of the main cathedral

Where to find Lothric, Younger Prince?

Lothric, Younger Prince is found at the top of the Lothric Castle

Where to find Iudex Gundyr?

Iudex Gundyr is found in the Cemetery of Ash, taking the sword from his body is required to start the fight.

Where to find Curse-rotted Greatwood?

The Curse-rotted Greatwood is found in the Undead Settlement, once the shortcut is opened the closest bonfire is "Cliff underside".

Where to find Crystal Sage?

The Crystal Sage is found in the Road of Sacrifices, on the left of the area when coming from the Halfway Fortress bonfire. You have to go through the building in which Orbeck is first found. There will be no fog wall to go through.

Where to find Deacons of the Deep?

the Deacons of the Deep are found in the Cathedral of the Deep, the room is located behind the cathedral's Apse, the furthest room from the main and front entry of a cathedral.
